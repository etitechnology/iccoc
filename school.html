Islamic School Initiative: Building Our Future

Establishing a premier full-time K-12 Islamic school in Orange County. We are bridging spiritual wisdom with world-class academic excellence to nurture the next generation of leaders.

Please note: This initiative is in its very early stages. We are currently in the first round of gathering donations and there is no building yet. For potential partnerships or to learn more, please contact us at contact@iccoc.net
