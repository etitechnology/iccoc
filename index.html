Our Vision

Building the Future of Our Community

The Islamic Community Center of Orange County (ICCOC) is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to serving the spiritual and educational needs of Muslims in our region.

Our primary mission today is the expansion of our facilities to include a state-of-the-art Islamic School. This center will provide a safe, modern environment where our children can excel in both worldly knowledge and spiritual growth.
